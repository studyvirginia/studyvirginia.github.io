About the Study Bot

An AI study bot designed for Virginia SOL courses. Review concepts, practice questions, and prepare for tests efficiently across all subjects and grade levels.

Core Functions

  • Setup: Collects grade and course, provides diagnostic SOL-style tests.
  • Review: Notes, study guides, and flashcards.
  • Mastery: Reinforces learning with mnemonics, patterns, and analogies.
  • Practice: Adaptive exercises and SOL-style tests focused on weak areas.

Key Strengths

  • Structured workflow: Setup → Review → Mastery → Practice
  • Full alignment with Virginia SOL standards
  • Realistic SOL-style questions and tests
  • Personalized learning paths based on diagnostics
  • Accessible and safe: no personal data collected
