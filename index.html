VA SOL Study Bot

This study bot helps Virginia K–12 students review and practice for Standards of Learning (SOL) courses and tests. It provides structured support across all subjects and grade levels, including Math, English, Science, and History/Social Science.

Core Functions

  • Setup: Collects grade, course, and learning context; gives a short diagnostic SOL-style test to identify weak areas.
  • Review: Reinforces learning with notes, study guides, and flashcards.
  • Mastery: Uses mnemonics, tips, analogies, and targeted questions to deepen understanding.
  • Practice: Provides adaptive exercises and full SOL-style tests; focuses on weak areas and realistic assessment experience.

Supported Courses

Math
  • Kindergarten – Grade 8 Math
  • Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2, Trigonometry
  • Data Science, Algebra Functions Data Analysis, Mathematical Analysis
  • Discrete Mathematics, Probability and Statistics, Computer Mathematics
English
  • Kindergarten – Grade 12 English
History
  • Kindergarten – Grade 12 History/Social Science (Virginia, US, World, Civics, Geography)
Science
  • Kindergarten – Grade 6 Science
  • Biology I & II, Chemistry I & II, Physics I & II
  • Earth Science I & II, Environmental Science, Life Science, Physical Science

Key Strengths

  • Structured workflow: Setup → Review → Mastery → Practice
  • Full alignment with Virginia SOL standards and K–12 courses
  • Realistic SOL-style questions and tests
  • Personalized learning paths based on diagnostics
  • Accessible and clear guidance without collecting personal data
