About the Study Bot

This AI study bot provides structured guidance for Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL) courses and tests. It supports concept review, practice questions, and SOL-style test preparation across all subjects and grade levels.

Core Functions

  • Setup: Collects grade and course; gives a diagnostic SOL-style test to identify weak areas.
  • Review: Reinforces learning with notes, study guides, and flashcards.
  • Mastery: Deepens understanding through mnemonics, tips, analogies, and targeted questions.
  • Practice: Adaptive exercises and full SOL-style tests; focuses on weak areas and realistic test conditions.

Key Strengths

  • Structured workflow: Setup → Review → Mastery → Practice
  • Full alignment with Virginia SOL standards
  • Realistic SOL-style questions and tests
  • Personalized learning paths based on diagnostics
  • Accessible, clear guidance without collecting personal data
